PYBOTMAKER FEATURES

PyBotMaker helps to build your bot with your own conversation design and principles.It provides text based conversational interfaces such as chatbot which can be connected with your website, mobile app and other platforms.

Conversation

PyBotMaker provides the feature of designing your own conversation model and interactions with your bots. It is completely depends on the developer. It is based on how the developer designs the conversation. The developer can create their own conversation design and principles and it turns everything into high quality and highly interactive views to the user.

Contextual

PyBotMaker is intelligent enough to know the exact current context and to response to the user, based on the current context. The entire conversation engine in the back end is completely optimized to analyze the context and perform operations. Each every operations are contextualized on the development phase itself.

Automation

The soul motive of using a chatBot is to automate the daily routine of a business. Here it enables every task of a business to be automated. It is development environment enables the automation by default. It helps you monitor every customer activity and satisfy their needs in an automated way.

Customer Engagement

With the cool environment and attractive user defined interfaces and amazing user defined conversation flow PyBotMaker chatBots always keeps the customer engaged. With amazing conversation engine and fast responsive method the chatBot really shines into the customer's Eyes. Your business keeps on increasing when you have your customers engaged always.

Customization

PyBotMaker allows you to develop your own customized chatBot. From templates to design and conversation and interaction, everything is depends on the developer. Every module can be customized according to the user need. Every entity and events are user defined and the developer has full control over the bot and can add as many as intents and conversations to the bot application.

NLP Engine

PyBotMaker has a full fledged natural language processing engine that understand all the user generated queries and responses accordingly. The natural language processing feature enables the chatbot to be more intelligent and responsive. Every kind of user generated queries are been answered and at the same time business growth and user engagement is guranteed on other hand.
